Character Creation

Want to make a character, but don't know where to start?

Here, you can add any notes you want, so you can easily come back to it later and modify it as you see fit.

Just write what you want to have as the basics for your creation. Hair color, race, age, height, anything you can think of, really!

    This is a work in progress, and hopefully things will be added to it later, where you will be able to get ideas for what you can add to your charcter design.
